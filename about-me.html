About Me

Hello! My name is Cameron and I am originally from Camp Hill, Pennsylvania. In 2019, two years after I graduated with a degree in Exercise Physiology, I moved out to Columbus, Ohio to pursue a career in fitness as a personal trainer. About a month after moving I was fortunate enough to find a job, but it was not in the exercise/fitness industry as a Personal Trainer. My new job was that of an eCommerce associate, helping the day-to-day operations of our company's WooCommerce and Amazon.com storefront's through data/cost analysis, product research, and inventory control management (just to name a few). At this new job, I worked with a full-stack web developer in our eCommerce department who had introduced me to programming. I was first apprehensive at the whole thing and thought to myself, "There's no way I could see myself getting into this. Yea, I've always loved technology and everything about it, but this just looked like something that was way too advanced for me". Boy, did my opinion change! I instantly fell in love with it. I found myself studying every night after work and practicing what I learned on the weekends. The idea of communicating with your machine and giving it specific instructions that would then be displayed in front of you was extremely fascinating to me. Fast forward to today, and I'm currently enrolled in a Full-Stack Java Bootcamp, pursuing a career in Tech that I never thought I would ever do. My passion for coding continues to grow every day and I'm excited for what the future may hold!
